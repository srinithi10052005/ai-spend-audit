# Economics

## What a converted lead is worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. A typical deal looks like:

- Company currently spends $500/month on Claude Team (5 seats x $30 = $150,
  plus API usage)
- Credex offers the same credits at 20-30% discount
- Company saves $100-150/month
- Credex margin on the deal: ~15% of face value

**Estimate of average deal size:**
- Target customer: engineering team of 5-15 people
- Average AI tool spend: $300-800/month
- Credex discount offered: 25%
- Average annual contract value (ACV): $300/mo x 25% margin x 12 = $900/year

**Conservative estimate: $500 ACV per converted customer**
**Optimistic estimate: $2,000 ACV per converted customer**

Using $800 as the working number for unit economics below.
