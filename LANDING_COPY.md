## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no login, no trial. You fill in your tools,
get your audit, and leave. We capture your email after showing you
the results — never before.

**Q: How do you know the pricing data is accurate?**
A: Every number in the audit engine traces to an official vendor pricing
page with a verification date. We update pricing weekly. The sources are
published in our open GitHub repo.

**Q: What do you do with my email?**
A: We send you a copy of your audit report. If your savings opportunity
is significant, someone from the Credex team may reach out to discuss
how discounted AI credits could help. You can unsubscribe any time.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude,
ChatGPT Enterprise, and others — sourced from companies that overforecast
or pivoted. The discount is real and substantial. This audit tool is free
because it helps us find companies who would benefit from Credex credits.

**Q: What if the audit says I am already spending well?**
A: We will tell you honestly. The tool does not manufacture savings to
impress you. If your stack is optimised for your team size and use case,
you will see a green "You are spending well" message. You can sign up to
be notified when new optimisations apply to your stack.

## Secondary copy blocks

### How it works
1. **Enter your tools** — tell us what AI tools you pay for, which plan,
   how many seats, and what you use them for.
2. **Get your audit** — our engine compares your spend against current
   pricing and usage benchmarks for teams your size.
3. **See your savings** — a clear breakdown of what to change, why, and
   exactly how much you save.

### Trust signals
- No login required
- Pricing data sourced from official vendor pages
- Open source audit logic — check our reasoning on GitHub
- Used by engineering teams at early-stage startups
- Built by Credex — the AI credits marketplace

### For high-savings cases
If your audit shows more than $500/month in potential savings, we will
surface Credex as a way to capture even more value through discounted
AI credits. No pressure — the audit is useful whether or not you ever
talk to us.
